**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

Skill Check ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit [www.skillcheckgame.com].

**1. Information We Collect**

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address via Supabase magic links.
- **Game Data:** We store your game progress, scores, unlocks, and history to provide cloud saves and leaderboards.

**2. Payment Processing**
We do not store your credit card details. All payments are securely processed by our Merchant of Record, **Lemon Squeezy**. You can read their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

**3. How We Use Your Data**

- To provide, maintain, and improve the game.
- To display your scores on the global leaderboard.
- To sync your progress across devices.

If you wish to request the deletion of your account and all associated game data, please email us at support@ki10games.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

**4. Contact Us**
If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **support@ki10games.com**.
